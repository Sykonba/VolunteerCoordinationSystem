In Home Support Volunteering Opportunity


Organisation: Cancer Council Liverpool
Location: Liverpool, UK
Description:
  • Be a part of a program that supports cancer patients and their carers in your local community
  • Make a real difference
  • Visit patients in their homes

The In-Home Support program helps people who have been diagnosed with cancer and their families deal with everyday household tasks such as light housework, dog walking, light cooking, transport to treatment or shopping, carer respite, or simply providing some friendly company. This free service is run by a dedicated team of volunteers who take great pride in putting a smile on someone’s face during a difficult time. We are seeking compassionate, personable and positive individuals to join their home visiting team.

Time Required:

Long Term, 1-3 hour visits, weekly (A typical visit is 1-3 hours long. You may be available for one shift a week or perhaps you’re available to take on several. We’re flexible! Those with the ability to commit to a minimum of 6 months will be highly regarded. )

Suitable for:
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Effective listener
  • Reliability and punctuality
  • Ability to show empathy
  • Resilience
  • A current drivers licence and comprehensively insured car
Essential requirements:
  • Working with Children Check
  • Criminal History Check
Interested in:
  • Helping and supporting others
  • Health
Additional Notes:

As an In-Home Support Volunteer you will make a difference to the people you visit. Once your application to the service is assessed and accepted, you will be allocated a time and date to attend a visit to a patient where you may assist with a variety of light tasks, or simply offer friendly companionship. In this role you’ll be a part of an innovative and forward thinking organisation where people are extremely passionate about supporting one another to achieve our mission. If you’re a like minded individual, we’d love to hear from you.
